Moussaka Recipe

Description

A Greek-style layered casserole with eggplant, meat sauce, and béchamel.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Ground lamb or beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Slice and roast eggplant.
  2. Cook ground meat with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and spices.
  3. Prepare béchamel sauce with butter, flour, milk, and eggs.
  4. Layer eggplant, meat sauce, and béchamel in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes until golden.
  6. Let rest before slicing and serving.
