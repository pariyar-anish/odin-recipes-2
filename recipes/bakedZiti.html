Baked Ziti Recipe

Description

A cheesy, oven-baked pasta dish with layers of ziti, meat sauce, and ricotta.

Ingredients

  • Ziti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook ziti until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and ground beef. Add marinara sauce and seasonings.
  3. Mix ricotta with Parmesan and some mozzarella.
  4. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  5. Top with mozzarella and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
